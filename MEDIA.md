## Tips
- JPG or PNG work fine. Keep files under ~500 KB each for fast loading.
- Landscape orientation looks best for the media rows.
- MP4 with H.264 is the most compatible video format. Keep clips short (~15–30s) and compressed.
- If you don't have a file for one of these, just delete the corresponding `<img>` or `<video>` tag from the HTML, nothing else will break.
