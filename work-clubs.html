Student Clubs

Clubs I Lead

Low-stakes, high-curiosity spaces for students to explore interests that wouldn't fit neatly into a class period.

Computer Club

Coding · Robotics · Digital Creativity

An after-school space where students dig deeper into whatever part of computing excites them, coding, robotics, game design, hardware tinkering, 3D printing. Club members have built VEX competition robots, games, and contributed to school-wide projects like Care Garden. The rule is simple: bring an idea, leave with something built.

Coding Robotics Maker Culture After-School

Camera Club

Photography · Visual Literacy

A club built around visual storytelling, photography basics, composition, editing, and the habit of really looking at the world. Students document school events, build personal portfolios, and curate student photo shows that rotate through the school lobby. The goal is to give students a creative practice with a low barrier to entry and a clear place their work can land.
