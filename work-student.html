Rams Care

Student PBIS App Live

Rams Care is a student-facing companion to our school's PBIS program. Students submit acts of kindness, self-reflections, and community contributions through a simple, friendly interface. Data feeds into a behind-the-scenes dashboard (see the Apps page) that helps staff recognize patterns and celebrate student effort.

Designed with middle-school usability in mind, big touch targets, minimal text, and a quick in-and-out flow so students can contribute during passing time or a homeroom check-in.

Guess That Teacher

Community Game In Progress

A lightweight web game where students try to identify teachers from baby photos, interview clues, or favorite things. Built to humanize staff, break down the student/teacher barrier a bit, and give the school community something fun to play during spirit weeks and assemblies.

Source code is open, I wanted other teachers to be able to fork it and run their own version at their schools.
